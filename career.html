Start your career with us

Are you looking for a rewarding career? We have lots of opportunities for personal development and career progression through our training and development programmes. A job in adult social care could be right for you.

You have the opportunity to make a difference.

Here's some of the highlights of working at Specialist Care Team . . .

  • We pay more – We believe in paying a fair price for an outstanding job for our team members. Our current rates range from £10.00 to £13.50 an hour, which depends on the level of responsibility you're happy with.
  • Flexible working – Whether you have regular driving lessons, play badminton on a Thursday night or need to look after a family member, we understand you may have commitments elsewhere and will always aim to have a flexible approach to your availability 
  • Guaranteed contract hours – We don't believe in 'zero hour' contracts – all of our team members have a guaranteed number of hours each week, which can range anywhere from 8-40 hours.
  • Going the extra mile – If you need to travel during work, no matter if it's using the bus or your own vehicle we will reimburse your travel costs (free bus pass issued or 40p per mile in your car)
  • Investing in you – You need the best tools to carry out a high level of care, and that's why you'll be fully-trained by our in-house team and members of our community team are given a work mobile phone so you can perform to the best of your ability

How you will make a difference

As part of our highly-trained team you will work with people to enable them to live happy lives, while promoting their independence. 

You will assist people with daily living, from personal care to meeting with friends and family, accessing the local community, budgeting, medications and daily household tasks.

You don’t necessarily need any qualifications or experience – what really matters to us is
that you have the right values.
