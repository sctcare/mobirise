About us

Specialist Care Team is an independent, locally-owned service with an office based in Morecambe. We have a core belief that mental health and wellness should be viewed on a continuum, with positive experiences and negative occurrences having the potential to affect us all.

We want to encourage people to be as self-managing as possible of their own mental well-being, recognising and challenging their own influencing factors. By offering individualised and innovative service delivery models, we are responsive to the flexible needs of our service users, while maintaining excellent levels of service quality.

We achieve the above principle philosophy by championing the following aims:

  • To provide the highest standards of care and support possible
  • To be reliable and dependable, while offering flexible packages of care and support, which are responsive to the needs and preferences of our service users. 
  • To ensure service users receive a level of support and encouragement, appropriate to their needs and preferences, which recognise and value them as an individual and promotes their diversity and beliefs.
  • To always encourage individuals to maintain as much of their independence as they wish or feel able to, while empowering people to be as self-managing as possible and to provide our service users with the support and encouragement to develop their ability and independence, to expand their daily living skills.
  • To nurture and enhance service user’s personal development, allowing them to develop their social skills and increase their independence. 
  • To use a person-centred approach when planning, delivering and reviewing all of our support services, to ensure that each service user is recognised as an individual, with their uniqueness valued, and their personal need and preferences catered for. 
  • To ensure our services offer value for money. We aim to offer high quality services, that are affordable and competitive within the marketplace, that meet the needs of our customers. 
  • To ensure our team is well trained with professional qualifications, and that they are confident, competent and well supported to enable them to offer excellent services.  
  • We have an overarching commitment to continuous quality improvement, and will constantly seek ways to enhance our performance and the services we offer. 
  • We will not discriminate against any group or individual on the basis of race, gender, origin, orientation predicament or situation.
