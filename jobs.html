Jobs

At Specialist Care Team you will work with lots of different people, for example someone with a mental health need, a learning disability, autism or dementia. Whoever you work with, you have the potential to make a massive difference to someone’s life.

This can make it a very rewarding role, and this is often what people enjoy the most about working in the sector. And it’s not all about working in ‘care homes’ – our social care workers are supporting people in their own homes, at work and in their community and family life.

We provide the highest standards of social care that ranges from intensive support packages for people requiring round the clock support, to specific task-focused support for a few hours at a time. We aim for recovery and put the individual at the centre of their own care and development.
